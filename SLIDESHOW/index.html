Welcome to my travel food illustration project.

This collection of illustrations began in 2018 when I took off traveling, spending time in my motherland â€“ Poland. Following a brief stint as a chef in Devon, I considered a permanent move to Poland, hoping to find work in an inspiring kitchen. Over the course of that year my home became Krakow, where I visited many restaurants, cafes and bars to learn about traditional Polish cuisine. But although my experiences were abundant, the local cuisine didn't fully grab me. And it soon became clear that poverty and homelessness was rife. Brexit was just around the corner. With a further situation brewing at home, I returned sooner than expected.

In order to capture those rare moments, I wrote about and illustrated my food experiences and many of the illustrations here are a record of that time.

Since returning I've continued to illustrate food, and find it useful to help me deal with the surreal, turbulant and ever-changing world I now find myself in. But there are times when motivation eludes me. So I hope that by giving these illustrations a permanent home I will discover my illustration-mojo once again and add to this collection. Perhaps one day I'll add a food-writing section too. And just maybe I'll be inspired enough to begin this process over again.

Smacznego!
